# BMA Digital Platform

## Hruaitute (Leadership Review) Tana Pawimawh Tlangpui

**Siamtu:** Technical Team  
**Ni:** January 17, 2026 (BMA Executive Committee Meeting)  
**Launch Hun Tur:** March 21, 2026 (BMA Annual Day)

---

## 1. Thuhma

Bangalore Mizo Association Digital Platform hi Bangalore-a Mizo khawtlangte tana communication leh membership hub pawimawh tak ni tura duan a ni. He app hian tun hmaa hmanraw hrang hrang (WhatsApp, Facebook, etc.) kan hman thinte chu hmun pakhatah (unified platform) a rawn khaikhawm dawn a ni. Saptawng leh Mizo tawng (Bilingual) a support vek ang.

**Thiltum Ber:** Technology hmanga khawtlang inzawmna tihchak leh Mizo hnam identity vawnhrin.

---

## 2. Features Pawimawh Tlangpui

### Member-te Tan:

- **Bilingual Interface:** App chhung zawng zawng Saptawng leh Mizo tawngin a hman theih ang.
- **News & Events:** Khawtlang chanchin thar, program hrang hrang, leh thlalakte awlsam takin a thlir theih ang.
- **Member Directory:** Bangalore-a Mizo awmte in-connect-na (Paid members chauh tan).
- **AI Help Desk:** BMA chungchanga zawhna apiang 24/7-a lo chhang thei tu AI chatbot (Mizo leh Saptawngin).

### Hruaitute leh Editor-te Tan:

- **Content Management:** Chanchin thar, newsletters, leh thlalak dante awlsam taka update-na.
- **Membership Management:** Member-te chanchin, payment status, leh renewal thurukte enkawlna.
- **Communication Tools:** WhatsApp leh Email hmanga notification thawn chhuahna.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** App hmanna report leh membership growth thlirpui na.

---

## 3. Sum leh Pai (Financial Value)

- **Cost-Effective:** He app siam nan hian vawi khat bak sum sen vak a ngai lova (one-time setup cost), kum khat membership fee tlem te atangin a enkawlna (maintenance) a daih vek thei ang.
- **Revenue Generation:** Membership fees lak khawm a tiahkhai ang a, advertisement leh donation-te tana hmun pawimawh a ni dawn a ni.
- **Efficiency:** Hun heu thram thin membership registration leh record vawnna hnate khawl (automation) hmanga tih a nih dawn avangin hruaitute hun a heu tlem ang.

---

## 4. Engvangin nge tunah?

- **Khawtlang Thruihawhna:** App hmanga kan in-connect hian member-te tan BMA hi "a tak" a ni zual dawn a ni.
- **Hnam Identity:** Kan tawng ngei app-a kan hman hian kan hnam identity a tichak dawn a ni.
- **Scalability:** Member 1,500 atanga 100,000+ thleng pawh harsatna awm lova enkawl thei tura siam a ni.

---

## 5. Hmahun Tlangpui (Timeline)

- **Jan 2026:** Design leh Architecture finalize.
- **Feb 2026:** App Development (Coding) leh Testing.
- **March 21, 2026:** Launch (BMA Annual Day-ah).

**Khaikhawmna:**
He platform hi modernization satliah a ni lova, BMA tana "Smart Business Decision" leh khawtlang tana "Long-term Investment" pawimawh tak a ni. Kan technology neih sa leh hruaitute remruatna nen, BMA hi digital khawvelah hma kan hruai ngei kan beisei a ni.
