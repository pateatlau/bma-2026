BMA Digital Platform

Executive Summary for Leadership Review

Prepared for: BMA Executive Committee Meeting Date: January 17, 2026 Proposed Launch: March 21, 2026 (BMA Annual Day)

Introduction

The Bangalore Mizo Association Digital Platform is a comprehensive mobile and web application designed to serve as the primary communication and membership hub for the Mizo community in Bangalore. This modern platform will replace fragmented communication channels with a unified, bilingual (English + Mizo) digital presence accessible to all community members.

Core Objective: Strengthen community engagement through technology while preserving Mizo cultural identity.

Key Features

For All Community Members

  • Bilingual Interface - Complete English and Mizo language support throughout the platform
  • News & Events - Stay updated with community announcements, upcoming events, and photo galleries
  • Member Directory - Connect with fellow Mizo community members in Bangalore (paid members only)
  • AI Help Desk - 24/7 chatbot assistant answering questions about BMA in both languages

For Leadership & Editors

  • Content Management - Publish news, events, newsletters, and manage photo galleries
  • Membership Management - Track memberships, payments, and renewal status
  • Communication Tools - Send notifications via WhatsApp and email to members
  • Analytics Dashboard - Monitor engagement, membership growth, and platform usage
  • Audit Trail - Complete transparency with detailed activity logs

Technical Capabilities

  • Multi-Platform Access - Web browser, Android app, iOS app - all from single codebase
  • Secure Payments - Razorpay integration for membership fees (annual and lifetime tiers)
  • Offline Support - View previously loaded content even without internet connection
  • Data Security - Bank-grade security with role-based access control
  • Scalable Design - Built to handle 100,000+ users without architectural changes
